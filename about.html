About Northern Access Floors

Northern Access Floors specialises in the supply and installation of raised access flooring systems throughout the United Kingdom.

Our business has developed a reputation for delivering safe, professional and high-quality flooring installations across a wide range of sectors including commercial offices, data centres, healthcare, education and public-sector projects.

We understand the challenges faced by contractors and consultants and pride ourselves on providing dependable support from tender stage through to project completion.
