Company overview

Northern Access Floors is a specialist supplier and installer of Tate raised access flooring systems for commercial construction projects. We work with main contractors, fit-out specialists, consultants, architects, public sector teams and end users.

Our approach is direct, credible and practical. We focus on competence, reliability and the ability to support live construction programmes — combining Tate product knowledge with practical site delivery.

  • Review drawings and specifications
  • Confirm suitable Tate system options
  • Provide a clear quotation
  • Coordinate installation with site teams
